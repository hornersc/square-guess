Square Guess

How To Play

Use the buttons to receive hints and guess the hidden word. Point value is shown in parentheses. Y is counted as a vowel.

One Player

Use the least amount of points as possible to find the computer's word.

Two Players

Each player must secretly input their word. Using the hints adds points to your opponent. Guess your opponent's word and gain the most points to win.
